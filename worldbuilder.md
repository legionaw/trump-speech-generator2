## Companies and Organizations ##

### Companies ###

* **SpaceX**: A private space exploration company founded by Elon Musk, focused on developing reusable spacecraft and advancing space technology.

### Organizations ###

* **New Whig Party**: A political party that emerged in response to the rise of Donald Trump and his "MAGA" rhetoric. The New Whigs advocate for a return to traditional values, strong national defense, and a more restrained approach to space exploration and colonization. They are critical of Trump's policies and rhetoric, and they seek to position themselves as a more responsible and pragmatic alternative to his leadership. The New Whigs have gained significant support among certain segments of the population, particularly those who are disillusioned with Trump's leadership but are not necessarily aligned with the Democratic Party. They have been active in organizing protests, lobbying for policy changes, and running candidates for political office at various levels of government. Formed in a bitter split from the Republican Party. Herbert Hoover, a prominent political figure and vocal critic of Trump, is the leader of the New Whigs and serves as their presidential candidate in the upcoming election. The New Whigs have been gaining traction in the political landscape, particularly among voters who are dissatisfied with both major parties and are looking for a new direction for the country.
* **Republican Party**: The traditional conservative political party in the United States, which has been significantly influenced and reshaped by Donald Trump's leadership and "MAGA" rhetoric. The Republican Party has experienced internal divisions and a realignment of its base, with some members embracing Trump's populist and nationalist agenda, while others have distanced themselves from his rhetoric and policies. The party's stance on space exploration and colonization has been largely shaped by Trump's vision of making the United States a dominant force in space, with an emphasis on military strength and technological superiority. The Republican Party has been a major player in shaping the political landscape of the story, with various factions vying for influence and control within the party as they navigate the challenges posed by Trump's leadership and the evolving political dynamics of Earth.
* **Democratic Party**: The traditional liberal political party in the United States, which has been the primary opposition to Donald Trump's leadership and "MAGA" rhetoric. The Democratic Party has been critical of Trump's policies and rhetoric, advocating for a more inclusive and progressive approach to governance, including in the realm of space exploration and colonization. The party's stance on space has generally emphasized international cooperation, scientific research, and environmental sustainability. The Democratic Party has been a major player in shaping the political landscape of the story, with various factions within the party advocating for different approaches to countering Trump's influence and advancing their vision for the future of Earth and space exploration.

## Galaxy and Planetary Setting ##

### Locations ###

* **Earth**, the home planet of humanity and the central hub of political, economic, and cultural activity in the story.
  * **The United States**, the most powerful nation on Earth and the location of the main characters and events in the story.
    * **Washington, D.C.**, the capital of the United States and the location of key government institutions.
      * **The White House**, the official residence and workplace of the President of the United States.
        * **Oval Office**, the President's formal workspace within the White House.
      * **The Pentagon**, the headquarters of the United States Department of Defense and the Space Navy.
    * **New York City, New York**, a major global financial and cultural center. Donald Trump's hometown.
    * **San Francisco, California**, a financial center and a hub for space industry and technology. Best known as a part of the Silicon Valley.
    * **Los Angeles, California**, a major center for entertainment and media.
    * **Paducah, Kentucky**, a charming small city and an important riverport on the Ohio River.
    * **Orlando, Florida**, a major center for tourism and recreation.
  * **France**, a country in the Western Europe.
    * **Paris**, the capital of France and a major global financial and cultural center.
      * **Elysees Palace**, the official residence of the President of France.
* **Luna**, the natural satellite of Earth and a location for various scientific research stations and mining operations. Also known as the Moon.
  * **Luna City**, the largest settlement on the Moon, serving as a hub for scientific research and tourism.
  * **Tranquility Base**, the historic landing site of the Apollo 11 mission, now a tourist attraction and research outpost.
  * **Tycho City**, a settlement near the Tycho crater, known for its mining operations and scientific research facilities.
  * **Mare Imbrium**, a large lunar mare that serves as a location for various scientific research stations and mining operations.
  * **Shackleton Crater**, a permanently shadowed crater near the lunar south pole, known for its potential water ice deposits and scientific research opportunities.
  * **Oceanus Procellarum**, a vast lunar mare that serves as a location for various scientific research stations and mining operations.
* **Mars**, the fourth planet from the Sun and a location for various scientific research stations, mining operations, and settlements. Partially terraformed, with a thin atmosphere and a mix of human and robotic inhabitants. Affectionately nicknamed "The Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance from iron oxide on its surface.
  * **Mars City**, the largest settlement on Mars, serving as a hub for scientific research and mining operations.
  * **Olympus Mons**, the largest volcano in the solar system, located on Mars. It is a popular tourist destination and a site for scientific research.
  * **Valles Marineris**, a vast canyon system on Mars, known for its geological formations and scientific research opportunities.
  * **Cydonia County**, a remote area with a small population, mostly consisting of scientists and miners.
* **Alpha Centauri**, the closest star system to the Solar System, located about 4.37 light-years away. It consists of three stars: Alpha Centauri A, Alpha Centauri B, and Proxima Centauri. It is a major focus of human colonization efforts in the story.
  * **Proxima Centauri b**, the first exoplanet discovered in the Alpha Centauri system, known for its potential habitability and proximity to Earth.
    * **Proxima Centauri Colony**, a small settlement on the surface of Proxima Centauri b, focused on scientific research and exploration.
  * **Proxima Centauri Station**, a research outpost orbiting Proxima Centauri b, focused on studying the planet's atmosphere and potential for life.  
  * **Alpha Centauri A**, the primary star of the Alpha Centauri system, around which several settlements and research outposts are located.
    * **Alpha Centauri Station**, a large space station orbiting Alpha Centauri Ab, serving as the main hub for interstellar travel and commerce in the system.
    * **Alpha Centauri Prime**, the largest settlement on the surface of Alpha Centauri Ab, serving as the political and economic center of the system.
    * **Alpha Centauri City**, a settlement on the surface of Alpha Centauri Ab, known for its advanced technology and vibrant culture.
* **Titan**, the largest moon of Saturn, known for its thick atmosphere and potential for scientific research and colonization.
  * **Titan City**, the largest settlement on Titan, serving as a hub for scientific research and mining operations. It is also a popular tourist destination due to its unique environment and stunning views of Saturn.
  * **Titan Base**, a research outpost on Saturn's largest moon, focused on studying its unique atmosphere and potential for life.
  * **Stress Management Retreat**, a luxury resort on Titan, known for its therapeutic environment and advanced stress relief technologies. It is a popular destination for high-profile individuals seeking relaxation and privacy.
* **Jupiter**, the largest planet in the Solar System, known for its massive size, strong magnetic field, and numerous moons. It is a major location for scientific research and mining operations in the story.
  * **Jupiter City**, a large settlement on one of Jupiter's moons, serving as a hub for scientific research and mining operations.
  * **Jupiter Station**, a relatively large space station orbiting Jupiter, serving as a hub of commerce and trade between Earth and Alpha Centauri, with a few thousand permanent residents aboard.
* **Rigel**, a bright blue supergiant star located in the constellation Orion, known for its potential for scientific research and colonization.
  * **Rigel Colony**, a small settlement on a planet orbiting Rigel, focused on scientific research and exploration.
* **Aether**, a planet located in a distant star system, home to the Kingdom of Aetheria, a powerful and technologically advanced civilization that has had significant interactions with Earth and its colonies. Their atmosphere is almost completely similar to Earth's, allowing humans to breathe without the need for specialized equipment. The planet has a diverse range of environments, including lush forests, towering mountains, and vast oceans, some of which have been shaped by the Aetherians' advanced technology and environmental management practices.
  * **The Kingdom of Aetheria**, a sole country on planet Aether known for their advanced technologies, including faster-than-light travel, energy weapons, and sophisticated artificial intelligence. They occupied a vast region of space and have a powerful military, but they are also known for their peaceful nature and diplomatic approach to interstellar relations. The Aetherians have a complex political system, with a constitutional monarchy and a parliament. The current monarch is King Alaric IV, who has been in power for several decades and is known for his wisdom, diplomacy, and commitment to the well-being of his people. The Aetherians have had significant interactions with Earth and its colonies, including trade, diplomacy, and occasional conflicts. They are known for their advanced technology and scientific knowledge, which has been both a source of fascination and concern for humans. The Aetherians have a unique culture that emphasizes harmony with nature, artistic expression, and intellectual pursuits. They have a rich tradition of music, literature, and visual arts, which are highly valued in their society.

## Races and Species ##

### Humans ###

#### Physical Description ####

Humans are a diverse species with a wide range of physical appearances, including various skin tones, hair colors, and body types. They have a complex anatomy with a highly developed brain, allowing for advanced cognitive abilities, creativity, and emotional expression. Humans are bipedal and have opposable thumbs, which enable them to manipulate objects and use tools effectively. They have a lifespan that typically ranges from 70 to 100 years, although advancements in medical technology have extended this in some cases. Humans are known for their adaptability and resilience, which has allowed them to thrive in various environments across Earth and beyond.

### Aetherians ###

#### Physical Description ####

For some reasons yet unexplained, the Aetherians are genetically related to humans, sharing 99.9999% of their DNA, despite the lack of contacts between them and the humans until recently, which has led to various theories about their origins and relationship with humanity. Because of this close genetic relationship, Aetherians have a similar physical appearance to humans, with a range of skin tones, large eyes, and slender builds.

#### Society and Culture ####

The Aetherians have a unique culture that emphasizes harmony with nature, artistic expression, and intellectual pursuits. They have a rich tradition of music, literature, and visual arts, which are highly valued in their society. Aetherians are peaceful people.


Aetherian society is highly unusual in that it tolerated a high degree of incestuous relationships, even if the members are already married to other people. The small difference between their DNA and human DNA prevented the negative effects of inbreeding, allowing them to maintain genetic diversity and avoid the health issues typically associated with incestuous relationships in human societies. However, somehow, sexual relationships between close relatives do not result in a pregnancy, which has allowed the Aetherians to engage in these relationships without the risk of producing offspring with genetic disorders. Even more unusually, sexual relationships between close relatives makes them more physically stronger, healthier, and more intelligent, which has led to a cultural acceptance and even encouragement of such relationships in Aetherian society. This has led to a complex web of familial relationships and alliances within Aetherian society, which has both strengthened and complicated their political and social dynamics.


The Aetherians have had significant interactions with Earth and its colonies, including trade, diplomacy, and occasional conflicts. They are known for their advanced technology and scientific knowledge, which has been both a source of fascination and concern for humans.
